How to Choose the Right Electrician

  • Credentials: Licensed, bonded, and insured
  • Care: Treats your property and time with respect
  • Communication: Answers questions and explains options clearly
  • Reputation: Look for reviews, referrals, and photos of real work
  • Local: Understands Michigan code and regional service needs

There are plenty of good electricians out there—but very few who check all the boxes. We built Top Tier Electrical to be the kind of company we’d want working in our own homes and businesses.

If you’re shopping around, that’s okay. We’re always happy to answer questions and help you make the best decision for your project—even if it’s not with us.
